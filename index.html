How much governance should we run vs. your team?

Governance is what keeps definitions honest as the business evolves — who can change a KPI, who approves a new source, how a mapping decision is documented. You pick the posture that fits your operating model.

Co-piloted
Managed

02 · C
Operations & maintenance
Can you run it? Do you want to?
Option A
You run it
Full handover. Your team owns monitoring, new features, and incident response.
In practice
A pipeline fails at 03:00. Your on-call engineer gets the Teams alert, checks the logs in Databricks, fixes the source issue, and reruns the job before the morning commercial standup.
For your business
Complete independence. No retainer costs after handover. But it only works if you have a capable data team — and the on-call commitment that comes with it.
Option B
We run it together
Shared ops. You handle day-to-day, we pair with you on new sources, upgrades, and tricky incidents.
In practice
Your team handles the Monday refresh and basic monitoring. When a new POS source lands, we pair with you for a week to onboard it. Quarterly accelerator upgrades are a joint exercise.
For your business
Lower cost than fully managed, with a safety net when things get tricky. Your team keeps learning, so dependency on us decreases over time — the growth path.
Option C
We run it for you
Managed service. We handle everything — monitoring, maintenance, new features — on an agreed retainer.
In practice
We monitor pipelines around the clock, fix incidents before you notice, deliver new features on a sprint cadence, and send a monthly ops report. Your team just sees Power BI refreshed on time.
For your business
Zero operational burden. Your team focuses entirely on commercial decisions. Cost is predictable and capped by the retainer, so there are no surprises at quarter-end.

Stage 01 · Ingest
Bronze — raw landing
What happens
Raw data lands from every source exactly as delivered. No transformations, no cleaning — just a faithful copy, with full traceability back to source for audit.
Tools & tech
Auto Loader, Delta Live Tables, Unity Catalog volumes as the source of truth store.
Master data role
Source-side product and customer codes captured as-is. Mapping to your canonical definitions happens later in the Harmonize stage.
Stage 02 · Clean
Silver — validated and typed
What happens
Schema enforced, types cast, duplicates removed, invalid records quarantined. Quality tests run on every record and fire alerts when expectations break.
Tools & tech
Delta Live Tables expectations, data quality rules, alert dashboards in Power BI or Teams.
Master data role
Potential duplicate product and customer records flagged for steward review in Eddy before they reach the harmonized layer.
Stage 03 · Harmonize
Gold — one set of trusted numbers
What happens
Source identifiers resolved to your canonical master data. Business logic applied, KPIs computed, hierarchies joined. The result: one set of trusted facts every consumer agrees on.
Tools & tech
SQL models (dbt-style), Delta tables, Unity Catalog lineage for full traceability from source to KPI.
Master data role
This is where Eddy earns its keep — every source-side product and customer code gets resolved to a single canonical ID, then joined to your hierarchy.
Stage 04 · Serve
Consumption — ready for the business
What happens
Harmonized data exposed through governed views. Power BI connects via DirectQuery or import, Chat analytics runs on the same tables, ThinkCell decks and exec packs all read from one layer.
Tools & tech
Unity Catalog grants, Databricks SQL, Power BI, REST APIs for downstream applications.
Master data role
Definitions are already resolved upstream — consumers see consistent names and hierarchies without ever touching mapping logic.

Common Questions

Frequently asked

What if our data is messy?

That's normal. The discovery phase maps exactly what you have. Our toolkit is designed for imperfect real-world data, not clean demos.

Do we need a dedicated technical team?

No. We build and hand over. Your team's involvement is reviewing business logic and approving matching suggestions — typically a few hours per month. If you want to grow the skills in-house, that's what Step 3 is for.

Can we start small and expand later?

Yes. Most engagements start with Tier 1 for one BU. You can add sources, BUs, up-skilling, or add-ons at any time.

What happens after handover?

Everything is yours. Optional ongoing support is available, but you are never locked in. If you have the team — or want to build one via Step 3 — you can take it forward independently.
