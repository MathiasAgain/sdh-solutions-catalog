The Problem

Fragmented data costs more than you think

FMCG organizations run on multiple sales sources that never agree. The result: wasted effort, unreliable KPIs, and slow commercial decisions.

Siloed Sources

Internal shipments and POS feeds live in different formats, cadences, and naming conventions with no common key.

Mismatched Hierarchies

Product names, pack sizes, and customer chains don't align across sources — making comparison manual and unreliable.

Manual Reconciliation

Teams burn 100+ hours per month stitching spreadsheets instead of generating insight and making commercial decisions.
